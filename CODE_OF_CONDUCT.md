# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This is a student robotics project. Keep interactions constructive and on-topic — focused on FEA, FDM mechanics, FTC robot design, and the codebase itself.

Specifically:
- Be direct and technical. Disagreement on physics or implementation is fine; make your case with evidence.
- Cite sources when disputing model constants. "I think the Z-stiffness ratio is wrong" is less useful than a paper that shows different values.
- Assume good faith. Most contributors here are students or mentors with limited time during the season.

## What's Not Welcome

- Personal attacks or dismissiveness toward students learning engineering
- Off-topic debate unrelated to the project
- Spam, self-promotion, or solicitation

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and any other channel where this project is represented.

## Enforcement

If something is out of line, open a conversation with the maintainer at micah.t.stoll@gmail.com. Serious or repeated violations will result in removal from the project.
